Brief Psychiatric Rating Scale, Expanded (BPRS-E)

History and guide

Based on the BPRS (q.v.), to which are added new items for schizophrenia (bizarre behaviour, self-neglect, suicidality) and mania (elevated mood, distractibility, motor hyperactivity).

Original: Lukoff et al. (1986), www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3810065.

Source

Lukoff et al. (1986), as above, and BPRS-E (version 4.0) at www.public-health.uiowa.edu/icmha/outreach/documents/BPRS_expanded.PDF.
