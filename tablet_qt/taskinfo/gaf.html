History

  • Health-Sickness Rating Scale (HSRS), Luborsky 1962 Arch Gen Psych;
  • then Global Assessment Scale (GAS): Endicott et al. (1976), PMID 938196, Arch Gen Psych (= American Medical Association);
  • then GAS modified to become GAF as Axis V of DSM-III-R (APA, 1987);
  • remains part of DSM-IV-TR (APA, 2000);
  • also modified GAF (mGAF), Hall (1995), Psychosomatics (= Elsevier).
