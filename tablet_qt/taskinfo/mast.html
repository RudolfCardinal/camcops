Michigan Alcohol Screening Test (MAST) and Short Michigan Alcohol Screening Test (SMAST)

History and guide

Original MAST: Selzer (1971), www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/5565851.

There are 22- and 24-item versions of the MAST.

The original MAST has weighted scoring (compare the UMAST, with one point per question).

SMAST (13 items): Selzer et al. (1975), www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/238068

MAST and related task validity in the elderly: Willenbring et al. (1987), www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3624732

Scoring (cut-offs): www.bookrags.com/research/michigan-alcoholism-screening-test--edaa-02/, Ross et al. (1990, www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/2270059)
