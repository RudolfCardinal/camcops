RAND 36-Item Short Form Health Survey 1.0

History and guide

Developed at RAND as part of the Medical Outcomes Study.

  • Medical Outcomes Study: see http://www.rand.org/health/surveys_tools/mos/mos_references.html
  • Ware JE & Sherbourne CD (1992): PMID 1593914.
  • See source below.

Note:

  • The RAND 36-Item Health Survey (as here, distributed by RAND) contains the same items as the MOS SF-36® (distributed by Medical Outcomes Trust / MOS Trust Inc.), but has a slightly different scoring system. See Hays RD et al. (1993), PMID 8275167.
  • A later, commercial version exists, the SF-36v2™; see http://www.sf-36.org/.
  • RAND use “SF-36” in the description of their version, but because of the trademark, we use “RAND-36”.
