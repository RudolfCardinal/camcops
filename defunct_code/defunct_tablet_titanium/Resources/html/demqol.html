Dementia Quality of Life measure (DEMQOL, DEMQOL-Proxy)

DEMQOL: 28-item interviewer-administered questionnaire answered by the person with dementia.

DEMQOL-Proxy: 31-item interviewer-administered questionnaire answered by a caregiver.

History and guide

  • Smith SC et al. (2005). Measurement of health-related quality of life for people with dementia: development of a new instrument (DEMQOL) and an evaluation of current methodology. Health Technology Assessment 9:1–93, iii–iv. www.pubmed.com/15774233
  • http://www.bsms.ac.uk/research/our-researchers/sube-banerjee/demqol/
