Altman Self-Rating Mania Scale (ASRM)

History and guide

Original: Altman et al. (1997), www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/9359982.

Scores of ≥6 identify mania/hypomania with sensitivity 85.5%, specificity 87.3% (Altman et al. 1997).

Source

From the STABLE Resource Toolkit at www.cqaimh.org/pdf/tool_asrm.pdf.
