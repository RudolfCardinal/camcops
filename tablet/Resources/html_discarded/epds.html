Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale (EPDS)

History and guide

Original: Cox JL, Holden JM, and Sagovsky R (1987). Detection of postnatal depression: Development of the 10-item Edinburgh Postnatal Depression Scale. British Journal of Psychiatry 150:782–786, www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/3651732.

Source

Cox et al. (1987), as above.
