Frontal Assessment Battery (FAB)

History and guide

Original: Dubois et al. (2000), www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11113214.

Low scores are worse.

In patients with early dementia (MMSE > 24), a cutoff score of FAB ≤ 12 (*) can differentiate frontotemporal dementia from Alzheimer's disease (sensitivity for FTD 77%, specificy 87%; Slachevksy et al. 2004, www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/15262742).

(*) I think: the phrase is "a cutoff of 12", which is somewhat ambiguous!

Source

From Dubois et al. (2000), as above.
