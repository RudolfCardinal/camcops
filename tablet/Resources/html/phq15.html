Patient Health Questionnaire-15 (PHQ-15)

History and guide

See Kroenke et al. (2002) (www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/11914441) and the instructions (www.phqscreeners.com/instructions/instructions.pdf).

The presence of ≥3 severe symptoms ("bothered a lot") identifies somatoform disorder with sensitivity 78%, specificity 71% (Kroenke et al. 2010, www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/20633738).
